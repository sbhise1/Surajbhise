    1)Noun

    Nouns are the words that are the name sof the places,Things,animals,People,Qualities,Ideas,Etc.Ex.Pune, Book, Tiger, Ravi, Courage, Etc.

    Kinds Of Noune

    A)Common Noun

    Ex.Boy,Man,City,Table.Book,Tree,Girl,Etc.

    B)Proper Noun

    Ex,Goplal,Ganga,Amit,Mumbai,Maharashtra.

    C)Collective Noun

    Ex,Class,Gang,Team,Crowd,School,Etc.

    D)Material Noun

    Ex.Gold,Milk,Wheat,Sugar,Oil,Silver,Etc.

    E)Abstract Noun

    Bravery,Truth,Pleasure,Honesty,Sleep,Etc.

    2)pronoun

    A)Pronoun is the word that is used instead of noun.

    a)Personal Pronoun

    i)First Person Pronoun

    Ex.I,We,Me,Our,Etc.

    ii)Second Person Pronoun.

    Ex.You,Your,Etc.


    iii)Third Person Pronoun

    Ex.He,She,it,They.Etc.


    b)Demostrative Pronoun

    Ex.This,These,That,Those,etc.


    c)Relative Pronoun

    Ex.Who,Which,That,Whose,Whom,Etc


    d)Interrogative Pronoun

    Ex.Who,What,Why,Which,Whose,Whom,Where,How,,When,etc.


    e)Common or Indefinite pronoun

    i)What do you want.

    ii)Tell me who come.


    f)Reflactive Pronoun

    Ex.Myself,Ourself,Yourself,Yourselves,himself,Herself,Itself,Themeself,etc.

    a)Main berbs

    i)Transitive Verbs

    ex.Kill, Made ,Open,road,Read,Speak,Etc.

    ii)Intransitive Verbs

    Ex.Swim,Smile,Gi,Come,Shine,Etc.

    Ex.i)The boys fly kites.

    i)Special Auxillaries

    ex.Am,is,Are,Was,Were,Have,Has,Had,Do,Does,Did,Etc.

    ii)Ex.Modal Auxillaries

    Ex.Shall, Will, ,Should, Would, Can, Could, May, Might, Must, Ough to,Etc.
